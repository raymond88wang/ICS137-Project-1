NanRay History

NanRay is an e-commerce company founded back in 2017 by founders Raymond Wang and Nancy Wong. The company started from the idea that consumers don't necessarily only want the latest trendy apparel, but perhaps some quircky ones or a mix of both. Therefore, NanRay was founded on the basis that consumers could easily locate and order whatever their quirky needs were on our website easily.

Team Members

CEO - Nancy Wong

Nancy Wong is a veteran in the world of fashion and cosmetics. With years of experience, she has the vision to discover all the trendy quirky mixes of current apparel our consumers crave. With her expertise and guidance, NanRay has a bright future ahead.
